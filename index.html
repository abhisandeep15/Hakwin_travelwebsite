Safety Guide

1.Choose Reputable Hotels: Your first line of defense is choosing a reputable hotel. Research customer reviews
and ratings to gauge the safety and security of your potential lodgings. Opt for hotels with good security measures in place.

2.Reserve Upper-Floor Rooms: Whenever possible, request a room on a higher floor. Ground-level rooms are more susceptible to
break-ins,while rooms on upper floors are generally safer.

3.Check the Door Lock: Before unpacking, inspect the door lock, peephole, and deadbolt. Ensure they work properly.
If you notice any issues, report them to hotel staff for immediate repair or room change.
